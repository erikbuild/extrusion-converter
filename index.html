Format reference

MISUMI

{family}{5 or 6}-{section}-{lengthMM}[-{alt}…] — e.g. HFS6-3030-1000-LTP-RTP or HFS5-2020-500-LTP

5-series prefixes: HFS, HFSB, HFSF, HFSFB, HFST, HFSTB, HFSH, HFSC, HFSR, HFSY, NFS, NFSB, NFSF, NFST, CAF.

6-series prefixes: HFS, HFSB, HFSL, HFSLB, HFSF, HFSFB, HFSLF, HFST, HFSTB, HFSLT, HFSH, HFSC, HFSR, HFSG, HFSP, GFS, GFSP, HFSY, HFSYB, CAF, EFS, NFS, NEFS, HFSMG.

Optional leading K (fixed-length stock) is dropped.

JLCMC

TXCJ-{surface}-{spec}-L{lengthMM} for 20-series, TXCK-{surface}-{spec}-L{lengthMM} for 30-series.

20-series (TXCJ) surfaces: H6 natural, H7 black, W1 white painted, B2 wood color.

20-series specs: 2020, 2020A, 2020B, 2020C, 2020D, 2020E, 2020H, 2020R, 2040, 2040A, 2040C, 2040H, 2060, 2080, 2525, 2550, 4060S, 4080S, 4420, 4420R.

30-series (TXCK) surfaces: H6 natural, H7 black.

30-series specs: 3030, 3030A, 3030B, 3030C, 3030G, 3030H, 3030T, 3030R, 3030RS, 3045M, 3060, 3060A, 3060B, 3060G, 3090, 30120, 30150, 5050S, 6060, 6630, 6630R with optional J prefix for Lightweight tier (J3030, J3060, J3090, J6060…).

Important caveats (always relevant)
  • End-tap depth, 6-series: MISUMI M8×24 vs JLCMC M8×20. Blind-joint bolts sized to MISUMI engagement may be too long for JLCMC's stock tap depth — spec the deeper tap by note or shorten the bolt.
  • End-tap depth, 5-series: MISUMI M5×15 vs JLCMC M5×12. Same caveat at smaller scale. For 25-mm-side sections (2525/2550) MISUMI uses M8×24 vs JLCMC M8×20.
  • Slot width, 6-series: MISUMI 8 mm nominal vs JLCMC 8.2/8.3 mm. M6 T-nuts cross-fit, but tight hammer nuts may feel loose.
  • Slot width, 5-series: Both 6 mm slot — M5 hardware cross-fits cleanly.
  • JLCMC 30-series RS radius (R30) ≠ MISUMI HFSR6 (R25). 5-series corner radii are smaller (HFSR5 ≈ R10) — verify JLCMC 2020R against the drawing.
  • Cut tolerance: JLCMC default ±0.3 mm (L≤1000) is tighter than MISUMI's ±0.5 mm — no alteration needed.
  • Material: JLCMC = A6063-T5 throughout. MISUMI HFS5 = A6N01SS-T5 / 6005A-T5; MISUMI NFS5 / HFS6 = 6063 family; GFS6 = 6061-T6 (no JLCMC equivalent).
  • JLCMC 20-series exclusive finishes: W1 (white painted) and B2 (wood color) — no MISUMI equivalent.
  • MISUMI 20-series HFST shape (T = 2-slot + 2-flat): JLCMC 20-series catalog does not list a T variant — translator falls back to the bare section and flags the gap.
